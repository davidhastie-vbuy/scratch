The Problem

No Trust

No Payment Safety

Homeowners pay upfront and hope for the best. No structured way to release payment tied to actual progress.

No Transparency

Quotes arrive via text or WhatsApp. No way to compare, no milestone tracking, no dispute resolution.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Hyper-local, postcode by postcode

Phase 1 — Seed Market

Launch in 3–5 postcode districts. Door-to-door provider onboarding. Targeted Facebook/Google ads to homeowners in those postcodes. Goal: 50 providers, 200 homeowners.

Phase 2 — Regional Expansion

Expand to full cities (e.g., Cardiff, Bristol, London boroughs). Referral incentives for both sides. Partnerships with trade associations and suppliers.

Phase 3 — National Scale

National marketing campaigns. Insurance & warranty partnerships. Premium provider tiers. Mobile app launch (iOS + Android).
